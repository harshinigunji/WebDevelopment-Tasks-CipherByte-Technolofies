Advil

Description

A drug used to treat fever, swelling, pain, and redness by preventing the body from making a substance that causes inflammation.

Directions for Use

Adults and teenagers—400 milligrams (mg) every four to six hours, as needed. Children over 6 months of age—Dose is based on body weight and must be determined by your doctor.

Precautions

If you have fever, headache, nausea, vomiting, and stiff neck or back while using this medicine, check with your doctor right away.
