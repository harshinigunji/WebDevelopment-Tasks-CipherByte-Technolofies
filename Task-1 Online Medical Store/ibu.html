Ibuprofen

Description

Ibuprofen is a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID) that treats pain, inflammation, and fever. It's available over-the-counter and by prescription.

Directions for Use

Always check the label. The usual dose for adults is one or two 200mg tablets or capsules 3 times a day. In some cases, your doctor may prescribe a higher dose of up to 600mg to take 4 times a day if needed.

Precautions

If you have fever, headache, nausea, vomiting, and stiff neck or back while using this medicine, check with your doctor right away .
